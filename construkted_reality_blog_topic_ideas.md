## Content Calendar Strategy

### Monthly Themes:
- **January:** New Year, New Projects - Goal setting and project planning
- **February:** Love Your Local Area - Community documentation focus
- **March:** Spring Into Action - Industry applications and case studies
- **April:** Earth Month - Environmental and heritage preservation
- **May:** Build Something Amazing - Creative and artistic applications
- **June:** Mid-Year Check-in - Platform updates and user success stories
- **July:** Summer Adventures - Exploration and travel documentation
- **August:** Back to Work - Professional applications and productivity
- **September:** Heritage and History - Preservation and historical documentation
- **October:** Innovation Spotlight - Future technology and advanced features
- **November:** Community Thanks - User spotlights and community celebration
- **December:** Year in Review - Platform evolution and future roadmap

### Content Mix Strategy:
- **40% Conceptual & Vision Content** - Building awareness and community
- **35% Instructional Content** - Helping users succeed with the platform
- **25% Technical Reference** - Supporting power users and enterprise customers

### Audience Segmentation:
- **Professional Content (60%)** - Industry-specific use cases and ROI focus
- **Hobbyist Content (40%)** - Creative projects and community building
- **Cross-Over Content** - Topics that appeal to both audiences

### Community Engagement Strategy:
- Monthly user spotlight features

This comprehensive content strategy provides Construkted Reality with a roadmap for building thought leadership, educating users, and growing a vibrant community around the vision of democratic, community-controlled 3D mapping.
